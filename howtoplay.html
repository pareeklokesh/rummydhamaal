LEARN AND PLAY RUMMY WITH RUMMYDHAMAAL

    Online Rummy is a simple card game to play and it’s easy to learn too. Indian rummy can be played both on 2 player tables or 6 player tables . It is a Game of Skill the half battle won when you learn the basics of the game. If you're trying to find a reliable and safe platform to find out the way to play and learn rummy card game then you're at the right place.

    These are the guidelines for you to follow after which you not only will learn how to play rummy games but also become an expert in this game.

  1. HOW TO PLAY RUMMY GAME ?

    2. Rummy is a simple card game that is played with two or more players. It requires 2 decks of cards if the number of players exceeds five. First player at a table arranges the cards which are dealt into predefined combinations and submits the melded cards. Your primary aim is to be the first player as the rest of the players accumulate points based on the cards in their hands. The player who has the least number of points at the end of a game wins the game.

  2. RUMMY CARD GAME RULES -
  3. Thirteen ranks of each of the four suits and additional 2 Jokers are included in 52 cards deck.
  4. Four card suits are: Clubs (♣), Diamonds (♦), Hearts (♥) and Spades (♠)
  5. Cards in each suit are Ace, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, and 10, Jack, Queen, and King. Here face cards are Jack, Queen and King are and Ace can be used either as 1 or as a face card when making the sequences.
  6. Joker: Jokers act as a replacement or substitute for any card in melding sets or sequences.
  7. Points associated with cards: Face cards – 10 points are associated with each face card and the rest of the cards have points according to the number printed. No points are associated with Printed Jokers or Wild Jokers.
  8. HOW TO PLAY ON RUMMY DHAMAAL

    9. You have to pick card from options namely– the closed deck or the open deck.

    Closed Deck : The remaining cards from the deck which has not been dealt to any player are closed deck. The topmost card on the deck, is unknown to the players.

    Open Deck : The pile of cards discarded per player in each turn is an open deck, these cards are visible to the players.

  9. RULES FOR PICKING UP CARDS
  10. You can pick only one card at a time when it is your turn.
  11. You can only pick the topmost card from Closed Deck or Open Deck, you can choose the pile.

  12. CARD COMBINATIONS
    13. There are two types of winning card combinations in a game of rummy when you finally meld your cards while placing a Show

  13. 1. SEQUENCES/RUNS

    14. When you have 3 or more cards of the same suit in consecutive order.

    For Example: Jack of Diamonds, Queen of Diamonds and King of Diamonds and can together form a sequence -J♦ Q♦ K♦ Similarly Three of Spades, Four of Spades, Five of Spades, and Six of Spades can together form a sequence - 3♥ 4♥ 5♥ 6♥

  14. 2. SETS & TRAILS

    15. When you have 3 or more cards of the same rank but of different suits, you cannot use two cards of the same value in a single set.

    For Example: Jack of Clubs, Jack of Spades and Jack of Hearts can together form a set - J♣ J♠ J♥

    Similarly Eight of Diamonds, Eight of Clubs, Eight of Spades and Eight of Hearts can together form a set - 8♦ 8♣ 8♠ 8♥

  15. PRINTED JOKER & WILD JOKER

    16. Jokers can be termed as wild cards as they play a very important role in the way your rummy game proceeds and in melding your cards as soon as possible.

    There are 2 types of Jokers in Rummy namely– Printed Joker and Wild Joker.

  16. 1. PRINTED JOKER

    17. Every deck of 52 cards has 2 extra cards which are known as Printed Jokers and they can be used to make sequences or sets once a pure sequence has been formed

  17. 2. USING JOKER TO FORM SET/SEQUENCE

    18. Below is how a Printed Joker can be used to form sequences and sets:

    Forming a sequence with Printed Joker: 8♦ 9♦ PJ J♦

    Forming a set with Printed Joker: 9♦ PJ 9♥

  18. SORT FUNCTION

    19. In Rummy Dhamaal the cards are distributed randomly between all the players and appear a whole block of 13 cards. You can easily arrange your cards by hitting the sort button available on the table. This feature sorts the cards according to their suits in increasing order of their ranks as shown. Using Sort makes it easier for the user to have a quicker understanding of what cards they have, strategize and decide how they want to proceed with the game.

  19. DECLARE

    20. When you have a valid meld and you need to declare your cards then you can proceed with declare button available on the screen.

  20. DROP & AUTO-DROP

    21. As soon as players have been dealt, you may be able to drop a game if you’re displeased with the cards you’ve received. However, this will give you a bunch of fixed and undesirable points. Drop is useful, if you are not able to meld cards to groups in next 3-4 rounds to lose less than 20 points, then you should drop the round with losing minimum.

  21. TIME BOUND

    22. The thing which separates it from offline rummy is that you cannot take your own sweet time for every move. This adds to the thrill of your skill.

  22. DiSCARD A CARD

    23. Select the card that you want to discard by clicking on it and hit the discard icon that appears above the card. The discarded card will then move to the Open deck and your opponent may either pick this card or pick a card from Closed Deck in their next turn.

  23. GROUP CARDS

    24. Select 2 or more cards that you want to group by clicking on them. After picking your combination, click on Group icon that appears on top of the cards. These are the combinations which are possible as per the above rules to declare 13 cards in rummy.
